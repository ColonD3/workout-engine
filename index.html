How It Works

This app uses rule-based workout generation based on muscles, goals, equipment, readiness, and preferences. It is not medical advice.

Each exercise has tags for muscles, equipment, pattern, fatigue, joint stress, difficulty, and goal fit. The athlete sliders bias the engine toward hypertrophy, max strength, neural/power work, jump exposure, tendon capacity, joint caution, density, and variation.
